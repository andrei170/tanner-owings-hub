Research Snapshot

5 gaps in the current funnel

The Five Gaps

  • No dedicated landing page. Cold traffic from content goes to a Discord invite. Zero conversion asset between.
  • No email list. Everything on IG/X/Threads. Owning email is non-negotiable at this stage.
  • No lead magnet. Industry standard is a free opener. Pre-Market Game Plan PDF is the obvious one.
  • Launch pricing is one-time. 38% off first 25 is a strong hook, but no ongoing urgency mechanism.
  • Podcast is untapped. The Day Trading Show has no clear funnel into the coaching community.

Highest-Leverage Fix

Build a landing page + lead magnet + 7-day nurture sequence between opt-in and Discord invite. Shift to quarterly cohort pricing for sustained urgency.
